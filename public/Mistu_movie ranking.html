The Best Movies According to Mistu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Main Hoon Na

This is my favourite movie. I love the plot and characters.

Baby

I love how the movie is short but has lots of action and smooth story telling. It keeps you hooked to the story till the end.

Golmaal

This is a comedy movie. Its all characters are funny and the ending has a plot twist which makes it even more interesting.
